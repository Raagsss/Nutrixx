Promote ProDentim — Earn with the ProDentim Affiliate Program

If you're looking for a product with decent appeal in the oral-health niche, ProDentim is one worth considering. Below is a breakdown of the affiliate program, what makes it attractive (and what to watch out for), so you can decide if it's a good fit for your affiliate efforts.

What is ProDentim?

ProDentim is marketed as a supplement (in this case a "melting probiotic candy") designed to support teeth and gum health. It claims to work via a blend of probiotics and nutrients targeting the oral microbiome (the good bacteria in your mouth) and is aimed at people age 35+ with concerns like bad breath, gum issues, weaker teeth, or wanting better oral hygiene.

According to the official website, ProDentim is a unique blend of 3.5 billion probiotic strains and nutrients backed by clinical research. A recent study published in Springer Nature found that people who have good teeth have a high population of good bacteria in the mouth.

The product addresses a common issue: many dental products (such as toothpaste and mouthwash) contain toxic ingredients that can destroy the microbiome in the mouth. ProDentim aims to repopulate your mouth with good bacteria through a unique blend of 3.5 billion probiotics and nutrients.

Key Ingredients

Inside every ProDentim you'll find 3.5 billion probiotics along with 3 unique ingredients that are clinically proven to support the health of your teeth and gums:

  • Lactobacillus Paracasei - Supports the health of your gums and helps your sinuses stay free and open
  • B.lactis BL-04® - Supports the balance of mouth bacteria, respiratory tract, and maintains a healthy immune system
  • Lactobacillus Reuteri - Helps with inflammation and supports a healthy mouth environment

Proprietary Blend of 4 Plants and Minerals

  • Inulin - Supports the good bacteria
  • Malic acid (in strawberries) - Helps maintain tooth whiteness
  • Tricalcium Phosphate - Supports tooth health
  • Peppermint - Natural anti-inflammatory

Affiliate Program Highlights

Here are some key features of the ProDentim affiliate offer that make it interesting:

  • The affiliate page states that affiliates are making around US $3+ EPC (earnings-per-click) on average
  • The offer is available via networks and platforms that specialize in health/nutra affiliate offers, with payout figures in the $80-$120 range for certain geos
  • Wide target audience: ages 35+, people with dental/teeth/gum issues, fresh breath concerns, even digestive/allergy overlap mentioned
  • Marketing support appears available: "Grade A VSL written by our top copywriter", "Crushes conversions across all channels", "Unique product format"

Why It Could Be a Good Choice for Affiliates

✅ Advantages

  • Niche demand: Oral health (teeth/gums/bad breath) is a steady concern, especially in older adult demographics
  • High payout potential: With CPA offers in the $80-$120 range (depending on region) there is significant upside if you can convert well
  • Marketing-ready assets: Having a well-crafted VSL (video sales letter) and conversion-friendly creatives helps reduce your work
  • Broad traffic possibilities: Because the audience is relatively large (35+, oral hygiene issues, bad breath, etc.), you may be able to run multiple traffic channels (blogs, YouTube, Facebook/Instagram, etc.)

⚠️ Risks & Considerations

  • Claim strength & credibility: Some reviews of ProDentim show negative feedback. Example: Trustpilot rating very low (1.6 out of 5), complaints about lack of results or refund issues
  • Regulation & compliance: Because you're promoting a supplement with health claims, you must ensure your marketing complies with laws/regulations (no unsubstantiated claims, misleading testimonials, etc.)
  • Refunds/returns & cancellation policy: High-ticket health/nutra products often have sizeable refund/return rates or subscription/continuity risk. As an affiliate, if refunds happen you might lose payout or have claw-back risk
  • Traffic cost vs ROI: Even though payouts are high, the cost to acquire that traffic may be high too, especially in competitive geos
  • Ethical promotion: Given the mixed reviews, you'll want to be transparent, avoid over-promising, and perhaps focus content on "helping your audience decide" rather than aggressive hype

Best Practices for Affiliates Promoting ProDentim

  • Create honest, helpful content — e.g., "5 things to know about oral probiotics for gum health", "Is ProDentim right for you?", etc.
  • Use SEO and content marketing: target keywords like "gum health supplement", "oral probiotic for teeth", "best supplements for fresh breath over 35", etc.
  • Use video & review format: video reviews, unboxings, user stories (clear disclosure that you are affiliate), before/after disclaimers
  • Split test traffic channels: blogs, social media, email list, YouTube etc. Check which geo(s) convert best
  • Monitor payouts, EPCs, conversions: The affiliate page suggests ~$3 EPC on average, but high variation could exist. Focus on ROI
  • Stay compliant: Don't make medical claims; include disclaimers; follow guidelines for supplement promotion in your region
  • Track refund and charge-back rates: high refunds mean less net payout; pick traffic with lower risk (e.g., content marketing rather than pop-ups)
  • Align with audience: Because the product targets 35+ with oral health concerns, adjust tone, visuals, messaging accordingly

Pricing & Guarantee

ProDentim is available in multiple package options with free shipping on most orders. The product comes with a 60-day money-back guarantee. If you are not impressed by the transformation of your gums and teeth, you can return what you haven't used for a full, no question asked refund.

Final Thoughts

ProDentim presents itself as a unique oral health probiotic supplement targeting the oral microbiome with 3.5 billion probiotics. While the affiliate program offers attractive payouts and the product addresses a real need in the oral health niche, potential affiliates should approach with realistic expectations, ensure compliance with regulations, and focus on ethical, transparent promotion that helps audiences make informed decisions.

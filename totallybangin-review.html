Totally Bangin Self Tanner: The Anti-Aging Self Tanner for 40+ Women

The only anti-aging self tanner for 40+ women that instantly hides cellulite, varicose veins, and uneven skin tone. Discover how this natural formula works with mature skin to create a gorgeous, natural-looking tan.

What Is Totally Bangin Self Tanner?

Totally Bangin' is the world's first self-tanner specifically formulated for mature skin (40+ women). Created by Carly Scott, a former personal trainer who noticed that traditional self-tanners were turning orange and streaky on mature skin, this product addresses the unique challenges that come with aging skin.

Unlike other self-tanners that get caught on bumps, lumps, stretch marks, and cellulite, Totally Bangin' is designed to work with these natural changes in mature skin, providing a smooth, even glow that lasts up to 11 days.

The Problem with Traditional Self-Tanners

As women age, their skin changes significantly. Traditional self-tanners often contain alcohol and perfume, which can cause several issues for mature skin:

  • Alcohol: Can cause buildup of dead, flaky skin cells where ingredients get caught, leading to that dreaded orangey stain
  • Perfume: Dries out the skin's surface and gets caught in creases, cellulite, stretch marks, scars, lumps and bumps, causing uneven, blotchy patterns
  • Short-lived results: Most tanners fade within 2-3 days
  • Poor coverage: Don't work well with the natural texture changes of mature skin
